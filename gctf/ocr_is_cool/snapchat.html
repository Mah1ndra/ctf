  1. Import Your Artwork

    Design in your favorite 2D or 3D software then import right into Lens Studio.

  2. Build Your Experience

    Bring your creation to life with movements, animations, and interactive triggers with our easy-to-use templates.

  3. Publish & Promote

    Submit your Lens! Once it's published, you'll get a unique Snapcode and Lens Link that anyone can use to unlock your Lens, or share it with their friends.
